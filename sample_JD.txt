Job Title: Junior Software Engineer (Python/Cloud)
Location: Berlin, Germany (Hybrid) Company: TechNova Solutions GmbH

About Us: TechNova Solutions is a leading innovator in sustainable energy management systems. We are looking for a motivated Junior Software Engineer to join our backend team in Berlin. You will work on scalable cloud services that process real-time data from IoT devices.

Key Responsibilities:

Develop and maintain RESTful APIs using Python (FastAPI/Django).
Collaborate with data scientists to implement data processing pipelines.
Assist in managing cloud infrastructure on AWS (Lambda, EC2, S3).
Write clean, testable, and documented code.
Participate in code reviews and agile scrum meetings.

Requirements:

Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related technical field.
Strong knowledge of Python and basic familiarity with SQL databases (PostgreSQL).
Experience with version control (Git).
Basic understanding of cloud concepts (AWS/Azure) is a plus.
Fluent in English; German skills (B1+) are highly desirable.
Proactive problem solver with a willingness to learn.

What We Offer:

Competitive salary and 30 days of vacation.
Flexible working hours and home office options.
Professional development budget.
A diverse and international team environment.
